  • Pilipino American Student Society

    Dominic is the current President of the Pilipino American Student Society. His goal for his term is to unite all members in the familial relationships at the core of this organization.

    The purpose of the Pilipino American Student Society shall be to unite the undergraduate Pilipino-Americans of Michigan State University (MSU) and to generate a greater awareness of Pilipino and Pilipino-American culture and heritage.

    The Pilipino American Student Society is a student organization at Michigan State that began in the year 1991 by seven individuals. Today hundreds of its members pride themseleves in the closeness that they share with each other.
